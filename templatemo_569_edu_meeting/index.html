Core Pillars & Value Systems

Our Operational Blueprint

Historical Background & Catholic Tradition

Established under the strict spiritual and management sponsorship of the Catholic Diocese of Bungoma, our institution honors the robust lifelong heritage of integrity, social justice, and educational investment established by the late servant leader, Maurice Cardinal Otunga.

The Student Prefects Council System

Governance at the student level is managed through a democratically audited council. Our leaders are trained in public speech, conflict resolution, peer mentorship and administrative assistance, ensuring standard student welfare across all class levels.

Environmental Stewardship (Green School Policy)

We run a sustainable campus ecosystem. Through student-led agriculture clubs, active localized tree planting initiatives, water conservation awareness campaigns and clean energy optimization programs, our girls learn practical climate responsibilities.

Academic Framework

Our Main Core Departments

Department of Sciences

Comprising Biology, Chemistry and Physics. Our well-equipped laboratory layout exposes students to rigorous practical analysis, hypothesis validation and annual Science Congress innovations.

Department of Mathematics

Driving quantitative accuracy, logic formulation and systematic calculations. The department hosts regular internal contests and registers good scores in the KCSE National examinations.

Languages & Linguistics

Nurturing professional communications through comprehensive English, Kiswahili and Foreign Language elective studies. We guide voice training, creative writing and public essay entries.

Department of Humanities

Exploring global sociology, history, geographical mappings and Christian Religious Education. This track establishes critical critical-thinking tools and ethical perspectives regarding societal development.

Applied & Technical Sciences

Instilling future-proof modern technical survival skills including highly interactive Computer Studies, standard Home Science practices and practical structural agricultural modules.

Talent & Leadership Cultivation

Co-Curricular Dominance

We strongly believe that structured education extends far beyond class boundaries. Cardinal Otunga Girls' High School is a highly competitive powerhouse in regional co-curricular championships.

  • Drama & Music Festivals: Award-winning regional and national sets focusing on local folk songs, spoken word compositions and orchestration.
  • Athletics & Ball Games: Exceptional indoor and outdoor setups for Soccer, Basketball, Volleyball, Handball, Badminton, Table Tennis and Lawn Tennis tracking professional fitness.
  • Clubs & Movements: Active deployments for St. John's Ambulance, President's Award Scheme, Young Catholic Students (YCS) and the elite Journalism Club.

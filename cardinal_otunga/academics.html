Our Academic Departments

Department of Sciences

Covers Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. We have well-equipped science laboratories where students do practical experiments to prepare thoroughly for their national exams.

Department of Mathematics

Focuses on helping students build strong skills in numbers, logic, and problem-solving. The team organizes regular mathematics contests and revision sessions to boost student grades.

Technical & Applied Sciences

Teaches valuable practical life skills. This department includes **Computer Studies**, **Home Science**, and **Agriculture**, where girls get hands-on experience in technology and farming.

Department of Languages

Teaches English and Kiswahili, which are core subjects for all students. The department focuses on public speaking, creative writing, and helping girls communicate with confidence.

Department of Humanities

Helps students understand society, history, and Christian values. This department teaches History & Government, Geography, and Christian Religious Education (C.R.E.).

Business & Creative Arts

Introduces students to financial skills and artistic talents. It includes **Business Studies**, **Music**, and **Fine Arts** to support both entrepreneurial minds and creative students.

Our Curriculum

We follow the official Ministry of Education guidelines, fully preparing our students for their KCSE national examinations. Our teachers offer extra guidance, evening remedial learning, and regular assessment tests to ensure every girl achieves her best score.

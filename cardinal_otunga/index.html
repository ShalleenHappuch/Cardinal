Our Mission and History

Our Mission

Our Vision

"To be a centre of all-round educational execellence."

Our Important Milestones

1972

Opened by the local community and Church as Kibabii Girls' Harambee Secondary School with just 37 pioneer students.

1973

Renamed in honor of Maurice Michael Cardinal Otunga, setting a lasting foundation of humility, faith, and dedicated service.

1982

Officially registered as a Provincial Girls' Boarding School, allowing us to welcome students from across the entire region.

2024

Elevated to a National School, proudly teaching and caring for over 2,550 brilliant girls from across Kenya.

Syllabus Highlight

A Glimpse of Our Subjects

Department of Sciences

Covers Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. We have well-equipped science laboratories where students do practical experiments to prepare thoroughly for their national exams.

Department of Mathematics

Focuses on helping students build strong skills in numbers, logic, and problem-solving. The team organizes regular mathematics contests and revision sessions to boost student grades.

Technical & Applied Sciences

Teaches valuable practical life skills. This department includes Computer Studies, Home Science and Agriculture, where girls get hands-on experience in technology and farming.

Life Outside the Classroom

Talents, Sports, and Activities

We strongly believe that a good education goes far beyond class boundaries. Our school is a well-known, highly competitive powerhouse in regional and national school championships.

  • Drama & Music: Award-winning performances in music, traditional folk songs, and public poetry that build deep student confidence.
  • Sports & Games: Excellent fields and courts for soccer, basketball, volleyball, handball, athletics, badminton and tennis to keep our girls active and physically fit.
  • Clubs & Societies: Active deployments for St. John's Ambulance, the President's Award Scheme, Young Catholic Students (YCS), Christian Union, Debate Club and Journalism Club.
Our Facilities

Our School Campus & Student Care

Clean Boarding Hostels

Our spacious and well-ventilated boarding houses offer clean sanitation, secure storage for personal belongings, and full-time care from our resident matrons.

Modern Computer Rooms

Equipped with modern desktop computers and high-speed internet where our girls can safely do school research and complete typing assignments.

Clean Dining Complex

A modern kitchen and dining hall serving healthy, well-balanced meals prepared under strict hygiene standards to keep our students focused and energized.

Guidance & Counseling Center

A dedicated, completely confidential department providing professional counseling, emotional support, and helpful life mentorship tailored for growing young girls.
